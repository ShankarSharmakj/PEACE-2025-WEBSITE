Conference Scope

Scope

PEACE-2025 is organized to provide a platform for researchers from various computing domains to share their innovative ideas. The scope of a conference covering Electrical Engineering. Artificial Intelligence (AI), Communication Systems, and Electronics is typically broad and interdisciplinary. These fields are increasingly interconnected, and such a conference aims to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing among researchers, professionals, and students. It provides a platform for sharing innovative ideas and addressing real-world challenges. Key topics include Smart grids, Renewable energy, Al-driven systems, 5G/6G communication, IoT, Embedded systems, and Recent technologies. The conference also emphasizes interdisciplinary approaches, integrating Al with Electrical, Electronics and Communication Systems to drive innovation in automation, networking and green technology.
